About

Happenin is free social app that helps you and your friends plan a night out. With the help of the app you will be able see which clubs in town are the most popular for a specific night.

By checking in to an establishment throught the app you and your friends can get special offers, such as free entrance and free drinks.

Besides offers the app also acts as a form of social media where users who are checked in will be able to upload pictures and comments, which are only visible to other users within the club/restaurant/bar that they are checked into. Everything uploaded is removed once the night is over.

While checked in you will also be able to send private messages to other visitors.

Establishments can also choose to let users give out freebies to users checked in at the same place. Its a great way to buy someone a drink and start up a conversation!

To use the app you will only need an facebook account to log in. So what are you waiting for, download it for free here!
