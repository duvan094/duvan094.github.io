The three most common roasts

Light roasts contain the most
caffeine, since roasting actually
burns caffeine!

Medium roasts exhibit a more balanced flavor and aroma than lighter roasts, but caffeine is somewhat decreased.

Dark roasts are oily and shiny and many are used for espresso blends, though they contain the least caffeine.
